Choose any of the properties of a card and search by them using the terms we've defined below. Searches are case-insensitive. You can use double-quotes to search along multiple words, and combine multiple search tokens.

Try these examples.

  • type:hero gt:"each non-hero target" q>=3 Try it!
  • kind:deck type:villain hp>3 hp<10 Try it!
  • power:damage complexity<=4 set:core Try it!
  • q=4 (g:collect or g:summon) Try it!
  • type:villain kind:character -gt:"start phase" Try it!

You can negate queries, use "or" to search for multiple things, and use parentheses to isolate certain groups.

General

t:, ti: or title:

Searches by a card's title.

g:, gt:, or gametext:

Search by a card's game text.

k:, kw: or keyword:

Search by a card's keyword.

ty: or type:

Search by a card's type (hero, villain, environment, event, critical event).

d: or deck:

Search by the name of the card's deck (e.g. Fanatic, The Organization).

c: or character:

Search by the name of the character (e.g. Haka, The Operative)

ki: or kind:

Search by the card's kind (a term we made up: deck, character, or event).

hp:, or hp:

Seach by a card's HP. You can also search using comparators, like =, >, >=, <, and <=.

f:, flavor:, or flavortext:

Search by a card's flavor text. This includes card quotes and event descriptions.

flavortextattribution:

Search by the attrition in a card's flavor text.

q: or quantity:

Searches by a card's quantity. Does not include character cards, so you can search by quantity=1 without tearing your hair out.

set:

Searches by a card's set (e.g. Core Game, Rook City Renegades).

back:

Searches by whether a card has a special back, like a hero character card, a villain character card, or an event card. Inputs include t, true, y, or yes for yes, and f, false, n, or no for no.

Heroes

innatePowerTitle:

Search by the title of a hero character card's innate power.

p:, ip:, or innatePowerEffect:

Search by the effect of a hero character card's innate power.

v:, or variant:

Search by the card's variant line (e.g. First Appearance, Dark Watch).

incapcaption:

Searches by the caption on a hero's incapacitated side.

incapfeaturedissue:

Searches by the featured issue on a hero's incapacitated side.

option: or incapoption:

Searches by the effect options a hero gets on their incapacitated side.

c: or complexity:

Searches by a hero's complexity. You can also search using comparators, like =, >, >=, <, and <=.

Villains

nemesisicon: or nemesisicons:

Search by a card's nemesis icon(s). There's no way to search for cards without nemesis icons. Ping someone from the Workshop in the discord server if this is something you want!

s: or setup:

Search by a card's setup text.

a:, or advanced:

Search by a card's advanced game text.

desc:, or description:

Search by a villain character's description (includes critical events).

diff: or difficulty:

Searches by a villain's difficulty. You can also search using comparators, like =, >, >=, <, and <=.

Events

date:

Search by an event's date. Right now this only matches on the text in "MMM YYYY" format (e.g. date:Jan, date:"mar 2000", or date:19 date:jun).

l:, limit:, or collectionLimit:

Search by an event's collection limit.

eventruletitle:

Search by a the title of an event's special rules.

eventruleeffect:

Search by a the effect of an event's special rules.

featuredissue:

Searches by an event's featured issues (e.g. The Savage Haka #1).

collectionflavor: or collectionflavortext:

Searches by the flavor text in an event's collections.

collectionfeaturedissue:

Searches by by featured issue in an event's collection.

rewardtitle:

Searches by the title of an event's collection awards.

rewardflavor:

Searches by the flavor of an event's collection awards.

rewardgametext:

Searches by the game text of an event's collection award.
